The Patron

Who is Prince Abdul Mateen?

Prince Abdul Mateen is the fourth son of Sultan Hassanal Bolkiah of Brunei — one of the world's wealthiest and longest-reigning monarchs. Educated internationally in the UK and Australia, he holds a military commission and is deeply connected to Southeast Asia's rising generation of leaders.

In 2024, his royal wedding became a global event — drawing heads of state, celebrities, and millions of social media viewers. But beyond his fame, Mateen is known for his genuine humility, love of sport, and his desire to use royal influence to lift up everyday people across Asia.

Application Process

How to Apply — Step by Step

Clear, fair, and fully online. No middlemen, no fees.

1
Confirm Your Country & Eligibility
Check that your country is among the 10 eligible nations and that you meet the personal and business criteria above. Use the online eligibility checker on the portal.
5 minutes · Free
2
Register on the Grant Portal
Create a profile with your name, country, business details, and contact information. You will receive a unique applicant ID to track your submission.
Email verification required
3
Select Your Grant Tier
Choose between the Seed Gift ($8,000), Growth Gift ($20,000), or Legacy Gift ($50,000) based on your business stage and intended use of funds.
One tier per applicant
4
Submit Your Business Plan & Documents
Upload your registration certificate, bank statements, national ID, and a concise business plan describing your vision and how you will use the grant.
PDF format · Max 10MB per file
5
Panel Review by Royal Advisors
The Prince's advisory panel — made up of regional business leaders and government officials — reviews every application. Shortlisted candidates are contacted within 21 working days.
21 business days
6
Virtual Interview (Shortlisted Only)
Shortlisted applicants attend a 20-minute virtual interview with two members of the advisory panel. This is your chance to present your business vision directly.
Online · Scheduled by email
7
Royal Ceremony & Gift Presentation
Winners fly to Brunei for the annual ceremony. Prince Abdul Mateen personally presents each grant as a symbolic gift on stage. Flights and accommodation are covered by the programme.
Travel covered · Annual event
8
Funds Disbursed + 6-Month Mentorship
Grant money is transferred within 5 working days of the ceremony. All recipients also enter a 6-month mentorship programme with senior advisors from the Prince's pan-Asian network.
Bank transfer + mentor access
